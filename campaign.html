A Cozy Hocking Hills Resort and Spa

Explore Our Logan, Ohio, Lodging Options

Our Hocking Hills bed and breakfast in Logan, Ohio, features a setting that connects guests with the harmony of nature and the simple joys of the Earth’s beauty. As a green-certified property, the Inn & Spa at Cedar Falls is surrounded on three sides by Hocking Hills State Parks. Mammoth rock formations, caves, and waterfalls are just a stone’s throw away from our 1840s Hocking Hills cabins in Logan, OH, geodomes, charming cottages, Pacific-style yurts, and Hocking Hills lodges. With so many options, you’re sure to find a Logan, Ohio, lodging option that suits your needs!

 

View Accommodations

Enjoy Dining at Kindred Spirits

Our Hocking Hills bed and breakfast offers guests a unique dining experience at Kindred Spirits, which is made up of two log cabins originally constructed in the 1840s. The open kitchen is the heart of the Inn, where guests may chat while watching meals being prepared from scratch. Fine American cuisine is the restaurant’s specialty and our chef uses locally grown ingredients where possible. Enjoy local flavors with the peaceful sights and sounds of nature when you dine on the seasonal enclosed patio. Join us in our tavern for an early glass of wine or a cold mug of locally brewed beer! Overnight guests at our Hocking Hills bed and breakfast can join us in the original log cabin for complimentary breakfast each morning of their stay.  

 

View Our Menu

Visit the Spa at Cedar Falls

A quiet retreat awaits those seeking luxury of spa services gently balanced with a closeness to nature. The Spa at Cedar Falls is inside an 1840s-style log cabin, which is the perfect setting for an afternoon of relaxation. The spa at our Hocking Hills resort pairs its woodlands setting with soft lighting and soothing music for ultimate relaxation. It’s truly an escape from the stress of everyday life! Open daily, the Spa at Cedar Falls offers a variety of spa packages, treatments, and gift certificates for men, women, couples, and groups. Call or book online today to begin your much-needed retreat at our Hocking Hills resort! Book online to receive immediate confirmation of your appointment. We can’t wait to pamper you!

The Gathering Place

The Gathering Place is a great event space in the Hocking Hills! This green–constructed, 2,500 square-foot, fully handicapped accessible space, features an open layout that’s perfect for group events. Our event venue is more than just a space to gather, though! Upon booking, one of our experienced event planners will create an unforgettable itinerary for your visit. Let them know your group’s interests and they will plan activities like organized hikes, wine and beer tastings, cooking and wellness classes, and more. Book the event space at our bed and breakfast in Hocking Hills, Ohio, for your next corporate retreat or social event of up to 50 people!
